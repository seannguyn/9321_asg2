1.  Install conda in your machine

                        https://conda.io/docs/user-guide/install/index.html

2.  After installation, in terminal, type:

                        conda create -n 9321_asg3 python

3. 9321_asg3 is the name of your enviroment

4. to activate or deactivate, in terminal type:

                        source activate 9321_asg3

                        source deactivate
